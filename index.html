Convolutional neural networks to identify câncers

The project applies convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to analyze Pap smear images for early detection of cervical cancer. By leveraging deep learning, the model classifies cellular images, identifying abnormalities that could indicate cancerous or pre-cancerous conditions.
